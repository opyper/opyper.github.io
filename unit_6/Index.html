Katherine Mansfield's baby meal: baked eggs with cheese straws

I came across this meal in Katherine Mansfield's journals (from 1919) and can vouch for the fact that it is very soothing and best eaten only with the company of a cat. I sat with Alfie(my version of Athanaeum) hiding from the storm outside and passed a pleasant afternoon consulting various cookbooks on the correct consistency of cheese straws and wondering why no one will give me a job. Follow your meal with a meditation on death, and a quickly jotted review of a Woolf novel and you're all set.
